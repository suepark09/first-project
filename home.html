Why Us

Famously “tempered over burning witches”, Holy Grale was originally created to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Monty Python, and is a much-loved part of our bottled beer collection. Some 19 years on, our ale has been given a new lease of life with the recent reunion and the subsequent Spamalot tour. Holy Grale ale is a light golden ale with a fresh, zesty taste and a long crisp bitter finish. It’s brewed with a mixture of hops, including WGV, which gives the ale a light fruity nose.
